Aglio e Olio

Description

A classic and easy pasta dish.

Ingredients

  • 100g of spaghetti.
  • 5 cloves of garlic.
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
  • 50g of parsley.

Steps

  1. Chop garlic into small slices, add to a pan over low heat with olive oil and toast for 5 minutes.
  2. While garlic is cooking, add pasta to salted water.
  3. While pasta and garlic are cooking, chop parsley finely.
  4. When pasta cooked, add to toasted garlic. Mix in chopped parsley and serve.
