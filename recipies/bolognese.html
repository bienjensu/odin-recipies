Bolognese

Description

Delightful bolognese.

Ingredients

  • 500g of low-fat mince
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 2 sticks of celery
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 2 cans of chopped tomatoes

Steps

  1. Brown mince until all water has boiled off, remove from pan.
  2. Dice onion and garlic, slice carrot and celery, and add to a pan with a tablespoon of olive oil. Cook until golden.
  3. Add mince, vegetables and chopped tomatoes to a pan. Cook for an hour.
  4. Season to taste. Serve with pasta.
