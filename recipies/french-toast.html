French Toast

Description

English-style french toast.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread.
  • 50g of butter.
  • 2 eggs.
  • 50g of milk.

Steps

  1. Heat butter in pan on medium heat.
  2. Crack and beat eggs on a plate, add milk.
  3. When pan is hot, soak a piece of bread on both sides in egg mixture.
  4. Cook both sides of egged bread until slightly brown.
  5. Repeat above two steps for second piece of bread. Serve and enjoy.
